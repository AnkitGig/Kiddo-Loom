Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Kiddo Loom? ▶
Kiddo Loom is a fun and engaging educational platform that helps children learn through creative activities and interactive lessons.
2. How do I create an account? ▶
You can sign up directly from the Kiddo Loom app or website using your email address or supported social login options.
3. Is Kiddo Loom free to use? ▶
Yes! Kiddo Loom offers free features for everyone. For a richer experience, you can upgrade to a premium plan to unlock more tools and personalized lessons.
4. How can parents monitor their child’s progress? ▶
Parents can use the Parent Dashboard to see detailed progress reports, learning trends, and recommended next activities.
5. What devices support Kiddo Loom? ▶
Kiddo Loom works on Android, iOS, and desktop browsers—allowing access from phones, tablets, or computers.
6. How do I cancel my subscription? ▶
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your app settings or by contacting our support team at support@kiddoloom.com.
7. Is my data safe with Kiddo Loom? ▶
Absolutely. We use secure encryption and follow strict privacy standards to protect your information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
8. How can I contact support? ▶
You can reach our friendly support team anytime at support@kiddoloom.com.
